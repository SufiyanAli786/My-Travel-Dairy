My Trips

Trip to Islamabad

Date: 15 June 2024 • Location: Pakistan

I visited Islamabad and enjoyed the beauty of Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh, and the Margalla Hills. The weather was pleasant and the food was amazing.

Adventure in Skardu

Date: 22 July 2024 • Location: Gilgit-Baltistan

Skardu was breathtaking! I saw beautiful lakes, mountains, and met kind local people. It was one of the best adventures of my life.

Beach Day in Karachi

Date: 05 August 2024 • Location: Karachi, Pakistan

A relaxing day at Clifton Beach. I enjoyed the sea breeze and sunset. The seafood dinner was delicious and unforgettable.
